A Serious Conversation

I want to talk to you calmly and honestly.

I care about you, and I feel peaceful when I talk to you. That is something very rare and valuable to me.

But I also believe that for any relationship to survive, we both need strength, clarity, and maturity.

If your parents pressure you for marriage, crying alone will not protect us.

We need:

  • Emotional strength
  • Courage to stand for our decisions
  • Focus on studies and career
  • Patience to wait until we are ready

I am still building my career. I need 2–3 years to become stable before thinking about marriage.

I don’t want you to feel pressure. I don’t want to rush. I just want us to grow stronger step by step.

If we are serious about each other, we must think practically, not only emotionally.

I respect you. I just want us to build something strong, not something rushed.
